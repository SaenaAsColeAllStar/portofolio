## Overview

SMK Teknovo Ecosystem is the first flagship case study for the CTOS Portfolio Platform. It is intentionally structured as a system story that can show problem framing, architectural decisions, and implementation trade-offs.

## Problem

The core problem is fragmentation: digital presence, communication, documentation, and operational workflows can easily live in separate tools without a coherent system view.

## Architecture Direction

The recommended direction is static-first public content with structured collections and selective interactive islands. This keeps the public experience fast while leaving space for future automation and workflow integrations.
